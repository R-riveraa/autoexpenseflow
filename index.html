How it works

Two steps. That's it.

The whole system is built around one idea — your time is better spent running your business than managing paperwork.

01

Snap a photo

When a delivery arrives or you buy supplies, take a quick photo of the receipt and upload it to your shared folder. That's your only job.

02

AI does the rest

Vision AI reads every line item, categorizes the expense, and updates your live dashboard within seconds. Throw the paper away.

03

Stay ahead

Your dashboard updates itself. If a supplier raises prices on any item, you're alerted immediately — before it affects your margin.
